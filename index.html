Design Choices:

  • Mostly achromatic with some pops of color
  • Accent colors in logo and video, blue-green and red, are complementary
  • Sans serif fonts, appears to have a range of four sizes
  • Bolded main heading, call to action, download button, and link headings
  • Clickable elements expand when the cursor hovers on them
  • Minimal content, focused on the demo video and download buttons
  • When video is clicked, it expands and blurs the background
  • Rounded shapes: video/image corners, buttons, socials
  • Color palette:
    #04ddb2
    #333
    #fff
    #767676
